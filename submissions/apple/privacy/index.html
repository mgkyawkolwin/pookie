Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-14

Pookie (“the App”) is provided by "Power 9 Inter Co., Ltd." as a free service for golfers at our resort. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user information.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics

How the App Works

The App is used only to randomly generate a T-off (starting hole) assignment for golfers for the next day. All processing is handled on our backend server, and the room number you submit is sent securely to our API to generate a random T-off assignment.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, advertising libraries, or external APIs.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Security

Since the App does not collect or store any personal data, there are no personal records to secure. However, we take reasonable measures to ensure the App is safe for users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. You are encouraged to review this page periodically for updates.
